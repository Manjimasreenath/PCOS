About Ultrasound Image Analysis

An ultrasound is an important diagnostic tool for PCOS. Our lab will perform the ultrasound and upload the images directly to our system for doctor review.

The procedure typically takes 30-45 minutes and is painless. You may need to have a full bladder for the test.

After submitting this request, our lab will contact you to schedule a convenient time for your ultrasound procedure.

What Happens Next?

Step 1: Lab receives your request and contacts you to schedule an appointment.

Step 2: You visit the lab for your ultrasound procedure.

Step 3: Lab uploads your ultrasound images to our system.

Step 4: Doctor reviews your images and provides analysis.

Step 5: You receive a notification when results are ready to view.

Preparation Instructions

For pelvic ultrasound: Drink 32 ounces (about 1 liter) of water 1 hour before your appointment and do not empty your bladder.

You may eat normally before the test.

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing to your appointment.
